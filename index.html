The Platform
What Is Valeri
Valeri is a full-stack underwriting operating system that transforms raw deal documents into complete, audit-ready credit analysis. Built on 25 years of commercial real estate lending experience, it combines deterministic financial calculations with AI-powered document intelligence to produce the same deliverables a senior analyst team would — at a fraction of the time and cost.
For Lenders
Elevate your screening and analysis workflow. Process more deals with fewer resources, maintain consistent credit standards across your team, and generate complete deal packages ready for investment committee review.
For Sponsors
Submit your deal documents and get back a full underwriting package — proforma, sizing, term sheet, and negotiation points. Understand exactly how lenders will view your deal before you walk into the room.
For Advisors
Handle larger pipelines without scaling headcount. Run preliminary analysis on every deal that crosses your desk, identify the strongest opportunities faster, and deliver more value to your clients.
Asset Coverage
Currently optimized for multifamily and mixed-use lending — covering bridge, value-add, and stabilized across the $10M–$100M+ deal size spectrum. Additional commercial property types on the roadmap.

Standalone Analysis Tools
Don't need a full deal run? Use individual analytical nodes on their own. Upload a single document — a rent roll, a term sheet, a sponsor package — and get back focused, detailed analysis in seconds.
Live
Term Sheet Comparison
Upload two or more term sheets. Valeri extracts every material term, aligns them side-by-side, flags discrepancies, and identifies the most favorable structure.
  • Rate, spread, and fee comparison
  • Prepayment penalty analysis
  • Covenant and reserve requirements
  • Recourse and carve-out mapping
Live
Rent Roll Analysis
Upload a rent roll in any format. Valeri normalizes unit mix, identifies concessions, calculates loss-to-lease, and derives market rent assumptions independently.
  • Unit mix normalization
  • Concession & vacancy analysis
  • Loss-to-lease calculation
  • Market rent derivation
Live
Sponsor Return Analysis
Upload a sponsor's business plan or proforma. Valeri stress-tests their assumptions, models return scenarios, and identifies where the plan is most vulnerable.
  • IRR & equity multiple modeling
  • Exit cap rate sensitivity
  • Business plan stress testing
  • Assumption gap identification
